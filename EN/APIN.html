What is IV NAD+ Therapy?

NAD+ (Nicotinamide Adenine Dinucleotide) is a vital coenzyme that plays a critical role in cellular energy production and supports mitochondrial functions. As NAD+ levels in the body decrease over time, cellular aging accelerates, energy drops, and cognitive functions weaken. IV NAD+ Therapy addresses this deficiency, strengthens the immune system, accelerates cell repair, and supports muscle and nerve functions.
